ABOUT US

Our story

Founded in 2010, Ameiz Lingua World was born from a passion for languages and a vision to create an educational environment where students could truly immerse themselves in new cultures while learning to communicate effectively.

Our founder, Emeribe stanley ameiz, a polyglot who speaks six languages fluently, recognized that traditional language learning methods often fell short of providing the practical skills needed for real-world communication. She assembled a team of dedicated language enthusiasts and professional educators to develop a methodology that emphasizes conversation, cultural context, and personalized learning

Today, we've grown from a small studio with just two languages to a comprehensive language school offering courses in over 12 languages. We've helped more than 5,000 students achieve their language learning goals, whether for travel, business, relocation, or personal enrichment.

Our vision

To provide exceptional language education through innovative teaching methods, authentic cultural immersion, and personalized learning experiences.

We are committed to helping our students achieve fluency and confidence in their target languages while developing cross-cultural communication skills essential

Our Mission

To create a world where language barriers no longer exist, and where multicultural understanding fosters global connections and opportunities.

We envision a learning environment where students don't just memorize vocabulary and grammar rules, but develop a genuine appreciation for the cultures behind the languages they're learning.
